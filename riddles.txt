1, What has keys but can't open locks? Answer: A keyboard
2, I am not alive, but I grow; I don't have lungs, but I need air; I don't have a mouth, but I need water to live. What am I? Answer: Fire
3, I am always in front of you but can't be seen. I am never touched, but always felt. I am never tasted, but always smelled. What am I? Answer: The future
4, What goes through cities and fields, but never moves? Answer: A road
5, The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I? Answer: Footsteps
6, I have a neck but no head, a body but no arms. What am I? Answer: A bottle
7, I am taken from a mine, and shut up in a wooden case, from which I am never released, and yet I am used by almost every person. What am I? Answer: A pencil lead
